Who Are We?

James Bond is a legendary British secret agent who has been captivating audiences for over fifty years. He is known for his charm, wit, and exceptional skills as a spy. Despite being in the service since 1952, he doesn't look a year older than he did when he first joined up just after the Second World War. Bond is proud to be the best, the most exciting, and the most seductive agent in the British Secret Service. He has had numerous thrilling adventures, facing dangerous megalomaniacs like Auric Goldfinger and Dr. No. Throughout his career, he has come face-to-face with death on multiple occasions but has always managed to come out unscathed. Bond's official designation is “007,” which stands for "Double-oh seven".
